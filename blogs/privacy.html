Last updated: March 20, 2025

Welcome to our Emotion Detection by Voice service. This privacy policy explains how we collect, use, and protect your information:

We value your privacy and are committed to protecting your personal information. This privacy policy explains how we collect, use, and safeguard your data when you use our emotion detection by voice services.

When you use our services, we may collect the following types of information:

  • Voice Data: Audio recordings of your voice to analyze and detect emotions.
  • Usage Data: Information about how you interact with our services, such as timestamps and usage patterns.
  • Account Information: If you create an account, we collect your name, email address, and other contact information.

We use the collected information for the following purposes:

  • To analyze and detect emotions from your voice recordings.
  • To improve and enhance our emotion detection algorithms and services.
  • To provide customer support and respond to your inquiries.
  • To send you updates and communications about our services.

We implement appropriate technical and organizational measures to protect your data from unauthorized access, disclosure, alteration, or destruction. However, please be aware that no security measures are perfect or impenetrable.

We do not sell, trade, or otherwise transfer your personal information to outside parties without your consent, except as required by law or to protect our rights.

You have the right to access, correct, or delete your personal information. If you wish to exercise these rights, please contact us at privacy@example.com.

We may update this privacy policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by posting the new privacy policy on this page. You are advised to review this privacy policy periodically for any changes.

If you have any questions or concerns about this privacy policy, please contact us at privacy@example.com.
